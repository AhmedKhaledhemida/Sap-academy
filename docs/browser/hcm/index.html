SAP Human capital management (End-user + Configuration) Course

The content of an SAP HCM (Human Capital Management) course typically covers the following topics:

  • Introduction to SAP HCM

    • Overview of SAP HCM module
    • Importance and benefits of SAP HCM

  • Organizational Management

    • Organizational structure and hierarchy
    • Organizational units, positions, and jobs
    • Creation and maintenance of organizational data
    • Reporting and analysis in Organizational Management

  • Personnel Administration

    • Employee master data management
    • Employee lifecycle processes
    • Time data recording and attendance management

  • Personnel Development

    • Competency management and development
    • Performance management
    • Succession planning and talent management

  • Payroll Management

    • Payroll processes and configuration
    • Payroll calculation and processing
    • Wage types and payroll schemas
    • Statutory compliances and legal requirements

  • Talent Management

    • Recruitment and applicant tracking
    • Onboarding and induction processes
    • Performance appraisal and goal setting

  • Time Management

    • Time Management Overview
    • Configuration of Work Schedules
    • Absences and Attendances

  • Reporting and Analytics

    • Standard reports and queries in SAP HCM
    • HR analytics and workforce planning
    • Integration with SAP Business Intelligence (BI) tools

  • Employee Self-Service (ESS) and Manager Self-Service (MSS)

    • Overview and configuration of ESS and MSS functionality
    • Employee and manager self-service processes
    • Employee and manager self-service portal
